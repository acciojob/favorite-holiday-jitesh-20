Kerela


Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is one of India's most captivating travel destinations. Located on the southwestern coast of the country, Kerala offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and unparalleled experiences.

  1. Munnar: Famous for its sprawling tea plantations and misty hills.
  2. Alleppey Backwaters: Known for its serene houseboat rides on the tranquil waters.
  3. Varkala Beach: A perfect spot to enjoy golden sunsets and pristine sands.
  4. Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary: Ideal for spotting elephants and experiencing nature.
  5. Fort Kochi: A cultural hub with a blend of history and art.
  • Appam: A soft and fluffy pancake, often paired with coconut curry.
  • Kerala Sadya: A traditional feast served on a banana leaf.
  • Puttu: A steamed rice cake layered with grated coconut.
  • Meen Curry: A spicy and flavorful fish curry.
  • Banana Chips: Crispy and golden fried slices of banana.
